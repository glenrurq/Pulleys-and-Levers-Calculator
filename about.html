Glen Rubén Rodríguez Quintero

Hello! I'm Glen Rubén Rodríguez Quintero, a 13-year-old student who loves computer programming, physics, chemistry, and math. I like coding in C and C++, which I use for Arduino and ESP32 projects, Clojure (my favorite), HTML, CSS, and this is my first time with JavaScript. I'm currently studying at the IES Miguel Servet in Zaragoza, Spain. This simple website is the second in a series of science outreach projects I'll be doing in the future, and the complexity will increase proportionally to the knowledge I acquire as I continue studying such interesting and engaging topics. The idea came to me when I noticed the difficulty my classmates were having calculating everything related to pulleys and levers, so I hope it answers your questions. I hope you enjoy it!
